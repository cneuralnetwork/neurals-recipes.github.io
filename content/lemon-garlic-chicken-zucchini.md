# Lemon-Garlic Chicken & Zucchini Skillet (Very Calorie Friendly)

This is high-protein, low-calorie, and uses minimal oil while still tasting good.

## Ingredients (1 serving)

* **Chicken breast:** 150–180 g
* **Zucchini:** 1 medium (200–250 g)
* **Olive oil:** 1 teaspoon (5 ml)
* **Garlic:** 2 cloves (optional but strongly recommended)
* **Lemon juice or vinegar:** 1–2 teaspoons
* **Salt, black pepper**
* **Optional spices:** chilli flakes, paprika, oregano, or Italian seasoning

## Steps

### Prep

* Slice chicken into thin strips or bite-size pieces
* Cut zucchini into half-moons or long slices
* Lightly salt the chicken (this helps browning without extra oil)

### Cook the chicken

1. Heat a non-stick pan on medium-high
2. Add ½ teaspoon olive oil
3. Add chicken, spread it out, don't crowd
4. Cook 3–4 minutes per side until golden and cooked through
5. Remove chicken and keep aside

### Cook the zucchini

1. Same pan, add remaining ½ teaspoon olive oil
2. Add garlic, sauté 10–15 seconds
3. Add zucchini, salt lightly
4. Cook 3–4 minutes until just tender (don't overcook; slightly crisp is best)

### Combine & finish

1. Add chicken back to pan
2. Add lemon juice / vinegar
3. Sprinkle pepper and spices
4. Toss for 30–40 seconds and turn off heat

## Nutrition Information

**Energy — 350 kcal**

This is the total calories. It represents how much energy your body gets from the food. Calories come from protein, carbs, and fats combined.

**Protein — 50 g**

Used for muscle repair, satiety, enzymes, hormones. Higher protein generally helps with fat loss and fullness. 50 g is excellent for a single serving meal.

**Carbs — 8 g**

Main energy source for the body. Includes sugars, starches, and fiber. 8 g is very low, making this ideal for low-carb diets.

**Fats — 5 g**

Important for hormones and vitamin absorption. Very calorie-dense (1 g fat = 9 kcal). 5 g is minimal, keeping this meal very calorie-friendly.
